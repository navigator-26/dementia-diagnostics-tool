About the App

Details

A person with dementia is trying to make sense of the world and to meet their needs, just like everyone else. Aware Spark responds to their challenges with trusted information, education and support services.Our sole purpose is to transform the experience of people impacted by dementia by elevating their voices and inspiring excellence in support and care, free from discrimination to have a positive change. We support vital research and equip communities with tools and knowledge to make our society more dementia friendly by helping the dementia patients or their caregivers to assess and detect their stages for progress. We involve people impacted by dementia in our work, to respect and reflect the diverse range of dementia experiences across the World, to reduce the stigma in the community. No matter who you are or how you’re affected by dementia, Aware Spark is happy to help!
